Who is Jey?

Jey Austen is a senior brand designer and illustrator from howdy and rowdy Austin, Texas.

With 9 years of creative experience across multiple industries – athletics, entertainment, and fintech, for starters – Jey has seen some shit, and loves learning new things and thinking on their feet.

Jey loves teaching creatives, and mentoring designers on best practices in Figma and Procreate. While you're here, be sure to say hi, especially if you're in education, activism, or entertainment!
